紡ぎ tsumugi — WASM actor, browser-local

No per-actor server. etzhayyim.com serves only the DID; the actor itself is a content-addressed WASM component fetched from IPFS and executed here, in your browser. Per ADR-2606014500.

Live equivalents: DID https://etzhayyim.com/actor/tsumugi/did.json · WASM https://etzhayyim.com/ipfs/<cid> (trustless gateway).

    ADR-2606014500 (one-Worker WASM-actor model) · ADR-2606013800 (dynamic did.json) · ADR-2606011800 (tsumugi). Inference stays Murakumo-only / ameno frozen-edge — this PoC is pure compute (edge-karma aggregation), no model.
